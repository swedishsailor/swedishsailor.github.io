About me


Hi!

My name is Kamil, I am 22 years old psychologist and game developer living in Gdańsk, Poland. My hobbies are: juggling, making PixelArt-style graphics, learning about human perception, watching anime and of course both playing and making computer games. I'm mostly focused on making games that I would love to play instead of treating the whole process like a key to have big salary.

In my opinion, only work interwined with huge desire of making something can lead you to real success and happiness. Furthermore I'm trying to spread the idea of making games less realistic and more artistic. I feel that trying so hard to do graphics as much realistic as possible, makes the game soulless and the work which should be dedicated to make interesting mechanics/world is used just for the visual aspect.

Let's make games! If you enjoy your project and treat it with love, other hearts feel that love as well! :)
