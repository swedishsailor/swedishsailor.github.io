Milion Lasers (Brackeys Game Jam)

Instructions: | Menu - use arrows to choose and enter to confirm | Game - Player1 (left) WASD to move, Player2 (right) Arrows to move
The most important - always keep an eye on the energy bar(bottom-right corner), you can't get too close and you can't go too far away from each other (as players), try to keep the bar on stable position. | PRESS BLUE SQUARE TO ENTER FULL-SCREEN Good luck :3
